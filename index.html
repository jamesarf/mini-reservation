How It Works

You can make a reservation in two simple ways ‚Äî either directly from the Available Slots card or through the Book Now form.

Method 1 ‚Äî Quick Booking from Available Slots

  1. 1. Browse the Available Slots card on the homepage.
  2. 2. Use the Prev and Next buttons to navigate dates within the booking range.
  3. 3. Click on a time slot to select it ‚Äî the slot will highlight in blue.
  4. 4. Click Book Now (below the card) to open the modal.
  5. 5. Your selected date and time will auto-fill ‚Äî just enter your name and email, then confirm.
  6. 6. The booked slot is removed instantly from availability.

Method 2 ‚Äî Booking via Form

  1. 1. Click Book Now from the main header or hero section.
  2. 2. In the modal form, select your preferred date and time manually.
  3. 3. Enter your name and email.
  4. 4. Click Confirm Booking to finalize your reservation.
  5. 5. The booked slot is saved locally, and you‚Äôll see a confirmation message.

Admins: You can adjust weekly working hours, slot durations, and date ranges directly in the Settings panel. The changes apply instantly to both booking methods.

How Admin Settings Work

Administrators can configure when and how users can book slots. The system repeats your weekly configuration automatically and applies your selected date range.

Step 1 ‚Äî Set the Booking Date Range

  1. 1. Choose between Fixed Date Range and Limited Number of Days.
    • Fixed Range: Pick a specific start date and optionally an end date.
    • Limited Days: Set a start date and define how many days users can book (e.g., 30 days from start).
  2. 2. You can check ‚ÄúUse today‚Äù to auto-fill the start date with the current day.
  3. 3. These date limits define which days appear in the Available Slots navigation and calendar picker.
  4. 4. Click Save Settings after adjusting the date range.

Step 2 ‚Äî Configure Weekly Slot Schedules

  1. 1. For each weekday, enter your working time ranges. Example: 9-12,14-17 means 9 AM ‚Äì 12 PM and 2 PM ‚Äì 5 PM.
  2. 2. Set a slot duration (e.g., 30 min, 45 min, 60 min). The system will automatically divide each time range into evenly spaced slots.
  3. 3. Leave a day empty if you‚Äôre closed that day ‚Äî it will be skipped in booking.
  4. 4. Click Save Settings once done. Your changes instantly affect all future booking displays.

Tip: All admin configurations are stored locally in your browser and applied immediately. You can revisit this section anytime to adjust your schedule or booking limits.
